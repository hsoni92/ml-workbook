Bird Topic, from the name you can
understand, is based on the Birt
transformer model.
It is a modern topic modelling
technique that uses sentence embeddings
to capture semantic meaning.
It also clusters documents based on
these embeddings. And extracts topics
from clusters using classical NLP
techniques.
The key idea here being that instead of
modelling word distributions directly,
word topic groups semantically similar
documents and then derives topics from
them. OK, so I'll see you in the next
