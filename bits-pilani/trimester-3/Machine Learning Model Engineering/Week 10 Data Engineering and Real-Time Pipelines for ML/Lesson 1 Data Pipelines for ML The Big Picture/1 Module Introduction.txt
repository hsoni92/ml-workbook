Welcome to Data Pipelines for Machine
Learning. The big picture. Up to now,
we have talked a lot about models
sawing, monitoring and retraining. In
this module, we zoom out to the data
plumbing that feeds all of that. In
this video, we will connect data
engineering to the machine learning
life cycle. Look at why a simple
notebook is not enough in production,
and introduce three ingestion modes
batch, micro batch, and streaming. The
core message is simple no data means no
model, and bad data means bad model, no
matter how fancy the architecture.
