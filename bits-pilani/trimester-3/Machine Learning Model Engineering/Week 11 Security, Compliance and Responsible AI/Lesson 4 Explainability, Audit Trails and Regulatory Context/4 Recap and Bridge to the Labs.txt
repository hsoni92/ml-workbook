To wrap up, explainability helps us
understand model behaviour at the local
and global level for debugging, trust
and review. Audit trails gives us logs
that tie together data, model version,
decisions, and time, so we reconstruct
what happened. The regulatory and
organisational context mean people will
ask why and how.
You need a system that can answer those
questions.
Design your machine learning system to
be traceable and interpretable enough
that you can talk about them with non
machine learning stakeholders, not just
other engineers. In the Model 11 labs,
we will bring this all together by
computing fairness metrics by group
turning them into simple fairness
checks and logging them along with
model metadata to form a basic audit
trail. That's a big step forward,
building secure, fair and accountable
machine learning in production. Thank
you.
