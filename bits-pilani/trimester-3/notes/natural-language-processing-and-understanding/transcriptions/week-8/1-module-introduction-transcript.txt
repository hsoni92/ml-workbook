10,000, maybe over 1000 words, right?
This also brings another problem called
the long distance problem. With LSTMs,
information from the 1st word usually
fades by the 100th word.
What if we could process the entire
sentence at once, instead of waiting
one by one? What if we threw away the
recurrence and relied entirely on a
mechanism that lets any word to look at
any other word instantly?
