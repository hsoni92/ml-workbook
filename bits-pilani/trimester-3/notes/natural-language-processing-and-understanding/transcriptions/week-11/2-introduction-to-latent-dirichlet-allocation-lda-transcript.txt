LDA mainly helps us answer what topics
exist in this text corpus and how they
are composed of these topics. LDA is
based on two simple ideas.
Documents are mixtures of topics, and
topics are distribution over words,
right, which means that a single
document can talk about multiple
different topics, and each topic is
defined by a group of words with
different probabilities. Let's see an
example to understand this further. For
example, a document may contain two
main topics, which is technology and
healthcare. And its distribution can be
about 70% technology and 30% healthcare.
Whereas the topic technology may
