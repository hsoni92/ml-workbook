Welcome to the 4 way tug of war,
accuracy, latency, cost, and user
experience. Up to now, we have talked a
lot about improving models, scaling
them, and monitoring them. In
production though, it is not enough to
simply have high accuracy. Real systems
are pulled in four directions at once.
Accuracy, how often the model is right,
latency, how fast the model comes back.
Cost, how much memory or money
the infrastructure we're burning, and
user experience, often shortened to UX,
how it feels for the users or the
downstream product. In this video, we
will build a simple mental model of
this tug of war and see why you almost
never get to maximise just one of these
without affecting the others.
