To wrap up, in this video, we have
framed production machine learning as a
four-way tug of war between accuracy,
latency, cost, and user experience. We
have seen that a pure latency mindset
is not enough. Lowering latency has
both user and cost implications. And
user experience is where all of these
decisions become visible.
In the next video of Module 8, Topic 2
will cover scaling pattern, vertical
scaling, horizontal scaling, auto
scaling. Topic 3 will explore cost
optimisation levers such as spot
instances, serverless inference, and
batching. Topic 4 will give you a
decision framework to choose the right
combination of model and infrastructure
for different scenarios. Then in labs,
you will compress a model, measure how
size, latency, accuracy change, and
practise deciding where that model fits
best on the edge or in the cloud. Keep
this 4 tug of war picture in mind. We
will keep coming back to it. Thank you.
