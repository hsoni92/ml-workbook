How often should we retrain? There are
2 main patterns, scheduled and event
driven. Scheduled retraining is
time-based, retrain every week, every
month, or quarter. It's simple and
works well when you have steady data
and labels. Event driven retraining
happens when drift monitors across
threshold, performance drops below SLOs,
or there is a major product or policy
change. In practise, you often use a
hybrid. A baseline schedule, so model.
Don't get stale Plus, event driven
triggers. That let you respond quickly
when something unusual happens.
Retraining is powerful, but it's not
free. It can be expensive, and a rushed
retrain can produce a worse model.
