Welcome to what to monitor metrics and
signals for machine learning systems.
In topic one, we talked about why
monitoring ML is different. A healthy
API doesn't guarantee a healthy model.
In this video, we will go one level
down and answer a practical question.
What exactly should we measure and log?
We will break it into 3 layers system
health, data health, predictions, and
business health, and finish with a
checklist you can reuse on your own
project. Before we get into specific
metrics. Let's anchor on the three
layers we are going to fill in. Layer 1,
system metrics. These answers, if the
service itself is healthy. Layer 2,
data matrix. These answers are the
input going into the model insane or
sane and stable.
Level 3, predictions and business
metrics. These answers are the output
is still useful, accurate, and fair.
Good machine learning monitoring needs
signals at all three layers. If you
only look at one, you will miss
important failure modes.
