To recap, in real production,
multimodal systems are the norm, not
the exception. Routing means choosing
one model per request, using simple
rules, learned routers, or fallback
models for safety. Ensemble mean
calling multiple models and combining
their outputs, trading more accuracy
and robustness for higher latency, cost
and complexity.
In the rest of Module 2, we will widen
the picture. We'll talk about sharding
and caching to scale these systems,
multi-tenant setups, and resource
isolation, and how vector databases and
retrieval augmented pipelines are
multimodal system by design. All of
this builds on the pattern we
introduced in this video. Thank you.
