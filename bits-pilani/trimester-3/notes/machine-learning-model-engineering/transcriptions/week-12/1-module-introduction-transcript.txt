Welcome to Module 12, Topic 1. Patterns
for Multimodal Systems, routing and
Ensembles.
Up to now, most of our examples quietly
assumed one model per service, one
endpoint, one model behind it. In real
production system. You almost always
end up with many models, different
versions running side by side,
different models for different user
segments or languages, and specialist
models for different tasks like fraud,
credit risk, and churn. In this video,
we will focus on one key question. When
a request arrives, how do we decide
which model to use? We will look at
simple routing strategies and at
ensembles, where we combine models
rather than pick just one.
