Welcome to feature engineering in
production, more than just a notebook.
In this video, we will briefly recap
what feature engineering looks like
when you are just working in a notebook.
Then we will see how things change when
the model has to run in production. Our
main concept today is training serving
skew, when features are computed one
way during training and slightly
different way during serving.
Your production model quality degrades
even though the offline matrix looked
great.
