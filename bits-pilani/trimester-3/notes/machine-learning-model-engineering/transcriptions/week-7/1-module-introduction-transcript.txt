Welcome to why standardisation and
optimisation Matter in production. Up
to this point in the course, we have
learned how to turn models into APIs,
containerize them, monitor them in
production, and even train and roll out
new versions. Now we are hitting a very
real world problem. This model work,
but can it run fast enough, cheap
enough, and all the hardware we care
about. In this video, I will show you
why the model we train in research
often don't fit production constraints.
Three big goals we care about in
production probability, latency, and
throughput and footprints, and a
preview of the tools we will use
standard model format, compression
techniques, and optimised runtime.
