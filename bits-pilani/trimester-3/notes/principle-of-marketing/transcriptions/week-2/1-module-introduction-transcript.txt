Welcome back to the module of marketing
foundations and strategic evolution.
This model introduces you to the
evolution of modern marketing concepts
from traditional marketing funnel to
modern flywheel models. You will
understand core frameworks such as Aida,
Pestel, and other foundational
principles that shape marketing
strategy. In this video, we'll step
back and explore the core learning
outcomes of this module.
You will begin by understanding
foundational marketing frameworks such
as ADA, Pestel, Porter's Fife, Forces,
and SWAT, which help structure
marketing thinking and analysis. We
will then look at the positioning
concepts, focusing on how to
distinguish between points of parity
and points of difference. And why these
are so important in competitive markets.
We'll also see how the SDP model is
applied in real world scenarios to
segment audiences, select target
markets, and position products
effectively. Finally, we will examine
how organisations manage multiple
products using the BCG growth share
metrics, the reason why different
products are positioned differently
across a portfolio. This model is not
just about learning frameworks, it's
all about building the reasoning needed
to make informed marketing decisions.
