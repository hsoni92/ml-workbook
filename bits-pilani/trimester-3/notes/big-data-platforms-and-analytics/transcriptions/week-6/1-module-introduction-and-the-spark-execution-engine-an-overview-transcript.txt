Its primary responsibility is
coordinating work across the entire
cluster. It acts as a bridge,
transforming your high-level Python,
Scala, or SQL code into a physical

primary components involved in this
process the driver programme. Think of
it as a conductor. It hosts the spark

DAG, which is essentially the master
plan of what needs to be done. Then the
cluster manager. This component acts as
the resource broker. Whether you are
using Yarn, MSOs, or Spark's built-in

job done, and then the executors. These
are the workers located on the
individual nodes of the cluster. They
are responsible for executing the
actual task, storing data in their
cache and reporting the results back to
the driver.
