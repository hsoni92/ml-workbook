They wait for the entire order. Why?
Because it's more efficient to
coordinate the cooking times and
resources once the full plan is known.
Spark actually operates exactly in the
same way. It waits until it sees the
full order of transformations, before
it actually starts moving the data. So
if Spark is just recording recipes,
