# Why DevOps Matters

## Business impact
- Faster release cycles mean faster feedback from users.
- Automation reduces human error in repetitive tasks.
- Smaller changes are easier to review, test, deploy, and roll back.

## Reliability impact
- CI catches defects before release.
- Progressive delivery limits blast radius.
- Monitoring closes the loop by showing whether release improved or harmed production.

## Exam examples
- A team deploying manually once a quarter has high release risk.
- A team with automated tests and canary rollout has controlled risk.
- DevOps success is measured by outcomes like lead time, deployment frequency, failure rate, and recovery time.
