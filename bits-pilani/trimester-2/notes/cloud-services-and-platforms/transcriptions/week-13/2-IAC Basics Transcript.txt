Let's now understand what infrastructure as code really means and why it plays such

A tool that reads that file and builds everything automatically. That is Terraform. So, a

different. ISE is like having a written recipe that anyone can follow and get the same
result every time.

That consistency is extremely important in the cloud environments. Before ISE, teams
struggled with issues like inconsistent environments, configuration mistakes and
difficulty recreating that exact setup. One developer's environment would work,

to make that happen. This approach also makes automation very easy.

networks, compute instances, load balancers, databases, IAM roles and even storage.

ISE is also a core pillar of DevOps.

Without it, automation becomes fragile and inconsistent. With ISE, infrastructure
becomes repeatable and scalable. So, the key takeaway here is ISE turns
infrastructure into something predictable, automated and easy to manage.
