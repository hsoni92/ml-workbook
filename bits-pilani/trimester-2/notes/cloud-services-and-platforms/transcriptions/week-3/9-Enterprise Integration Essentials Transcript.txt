enterprises do not start from zero. They already have identity systems, business
applications, compliance rules, networks, and operational processes in place.

doesn't integrate well with an organisation's tools and workflows, adoption becomes
slow and painful.

Starting with AWS. AWS has the broadest and deepest cloud service ecosystem in

This works very well for technology -first companies like SaaS platforms, and also the

Azure is extremely strong in enterprise environments, especially those already using
