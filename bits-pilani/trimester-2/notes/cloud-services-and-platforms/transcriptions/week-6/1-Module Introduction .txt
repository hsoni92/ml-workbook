AWS, networking is completely software-defined.
There are no physical cables for us to touch. But the same ideas still exist, which is
private networks, IP addresses, routing rules, and security boundaries. So, a VPC is
essentially your own private network inside AWS.

can reach the internet, and which ones must stay completely private. So, in this
