Yudhishthira had to experience hell for only a moment, because of his lie to

• 
According to one tradition, there are six planes of existence (lokas) above 
earth and seven lokas (hells) below. However, no action can occur in these 
other worlds, so that a person's karma doesn't change until he returns to 
earth.   
• 
“Actions performed in accordance with scriptural injunctions … lead the 
performer to the heavenly planets for prolonged sensual enjoyment. 
However, when a person's pious credits are exhausted, he must return to 
Earth, just as a person returns from a holiday and resumes his work.” (“BG 
as it is: Online”)
