Light Amplification: 
Light amplification requires stimulated emission exclusively. In practice, absorption 
and spontaneous emission always occur together with stimulated emission. The laser operation 
is achieved when stimulated emission exceeds the other two processes due to its higher 
transitions rates of atomic energy levels.
