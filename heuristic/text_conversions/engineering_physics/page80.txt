ATTENUATION (POWER-LOSS) IN OPTICAL FIBERS 
When light propagates through an optical fiber, then the power of the light at the output end is 
found to be always less than the power launched at the input end. The loss of power is called 
Attenuation. It is measured in terms of decibels per kilometer.
