1. <span style="color:#FFA07A">**D**</span>etection: Recognizing patterns or values
2. <span style="color:#FF6B6B">**A**</span>ssembly: Grouping geometric objects to understand relationships
3. <span style="color:#98D8C8">**E**</span>stimation: Assessing magnitude or comparisons

- <span style="color:#3498DB">**P**</span>osition on Common Scale: Most effective
- <span style="color:#3498DB">**P**</span>osition on Identical but Non-Aligned Scales: Effective
- <span style="color:#5DADE2">**L**</span>ength: Very effective
- <span style="color:#FF6B6B">**A**</span>ngle: Useful for ranking or estimation
- <span style="color:#16A085">**V**</span>olume / Density / Saturation: Useful but less effective
- <span style="color:#45B7D1">**C**</span>olor: Best for discrimination, not ranking
